Controls

KEYBOARD

  • Z - charge up a jump
  • X - reroll Froge meme (titlescreen)

Jump over hurdles, snowmen, and much more to escape the evil red frog! The game ends when he leaves the right edge of your screen.

Stretch from platform to platform to reclaim your treasure and move to the exit to escape before the assorted spooks can steal it back.

Froge

Development

"Froge" is a simple racing game developed as a cart for PICO-8.

Michael Knight developed it on his own. This is his first solo game development project. Froge was programmed in a week. All of the assets used were created for Froge in PICO-8's vfx / sfx creator.
