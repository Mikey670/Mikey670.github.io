Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to inform the user about my game development company, and the games that we’ve made. I already have a company, and we’ve been working on our first game. The website will contain information about all of our games, be those that are currently in development, or ones that are already published. The website will feature links to where these games can be purchased, downloaded, or even played online.

Audience

My target audience will be young adults who like to play video games. Most people who will enjoy the game that I am developing will have prior experience playing video games.
