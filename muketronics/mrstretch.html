Controls

KEYBOARD

  • Tap WASD/Arrows - Move
  • Q - Use Binoculars
  • Esc - Pause/Unpause
  • R - Restart level

CONTROLLER

  • Tap Left Stick/D-pad - Move
  • Y - Use Binoculars
  • Start - Pause/Unpause
  • Select - Restart level

Join the late Mr. Stretch on his quest to reclaim his fortune, stolen from him by an old enemy that Mr. Stretch can't quite remember. Mr. Stretch must return to his old manor and gather up all of his treasure so that he can return it to his tomb and bask in its brilliance. There must be more than meets the eye to this theft, but Mr. Stretch's memory is getting foggy as he grows older. All Mr. Stretch knows is that he'll do anything to get his fortune back.
Stretch from platform to platform to reclaim your treasure and move to the exit to escape before the assorted spooks can steal it back.
Uncover Secrets about the past and present by collecting elusive journal pages.
Beat the game the hard way by puzzling out gem combos and besting par scores.
This Demo includes the first 20 levels of exciting gameplay and one of the secret Artifact Vaults.
